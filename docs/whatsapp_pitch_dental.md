## Sending rules (important)

1. **Max 15–20 messages/day per WhatsApp number.** Bulk senders get flagged.
2. **Personalize every message** — replace `{Clinic Name}`, `{reviews}`, `{rating}`, `{locality}`. Pre-personalized versions are in `leads/dental_ghaziabad_messages.md`.
3. **Best send windows:** 10 AM – 1 PM or 5 PM – 8 PM. Clinic front-desks are most responsive then.
4. **Follow-up once after 48 hours** if no reply. Then drop it — don't be a pest.
5. **If WhatsApp flags you:** switch to a second number, or send from your phone manually (not Web).

## Objections + quick replies

**"Practo already brings us patients, why do we need a site?"**
> That's exactly the point — Practo charges 10-15% commission on every booking. Your own site captures bookings with zero commission, and patients who search your clinic name directly will still find *you*, not a Practo listing for your competitor.

**"Do you have other clinics as clients?"**
> You'd be one of my founding 3, which is why the price is ₹3,999 instead of ₹11,999. I'll show you the live sample — if you're happy with that quality, we can start. If not, no pressure.

**"Can we see more samples?"**
> The sample I sent is the exact template I'll customize for you — your logo, your colors, your services, your doctors. Happy to walk you through it on a 2-min call.

**"Can you do it in 2 days instead of 3?"**
> Yes, if you send me content (photos, services list) same-day. 3 days is a buffer for revisions.

**"Too expensive."**
> Compared to what? A Practo Pro listing is ₹2,000+/month (₹24k/year). This is ₹3,999 one-time — you own it forever.
