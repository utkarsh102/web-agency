# Niche Presets

Each preset gives Claude an instant starting point — the visual personality, services to feature, stats to include, testimonial archetypes, image niches, and trust signals that match the industry. Use these as a launching pad, not a cage.

---

## Clinic / Medical Practice

- **Default personality**: Editorial Light or Clinical Sharp
- **Aesthetic tone**: Editorial/Magazine — calm, premium, trustworthy
- **Hero headline templates**:
  - "Care that listens."
  - "Health, on your terms."
  - "[Speciality] done right, in [city]."
- **Services to feature**: Whole-body diagnostics, Specialist consultations, Preventive screenings, Telehealth, Lab tests, Pharmacy
- **Stats**: Patients served, Years in practice, Specialists on staff, Average wait time
- **Trust signals**: NABH accreditation, partner insurance logos, "Reviewed by X+ patients on Practo"
- **Testimonial archetype**: Patient mentions a specific doctor, a result, and emotional relief
- **Image niche IDs** (from SKILL.md table): clinic / medical
- **Sections to emphasize**: Process (Book → Visit → Care → Follow-up), Doctor Bios (replace one bento tile)
- **CTA verbs**: "Book a Consultation", "Find Your Specialist", "Schedule a Visit"

## Default for Unknown Niche

If the client doesn't fit any preset above:
1. Pick **Editorial Light** as personality (works for almost any business)
2. Use **Fraunces + Geist** as the font pair
3. Keep all 10 sections from SKILL.md
4. Ask the user for: 3–5 services, 4 stats they're proud of, 3 testimonial archetypes
